about us

we are expert movie enthusiasts

We are aiming at providing you with the ultimate resource to find what is worth watching and where to watch it. Including user based ratings, short plots and links to trailers, we will give you the ultimate door to the world of internet movies.

where we came from

We started as a start-up out of university, as the brain-child of two BTU students of computer science. We are located in Cottbus very close to one of the biggest cultural hot spots of germany (Berlin).

where we are going

Our goal is to be a consistent help to navigate through the vast jungle of online entertainment. For you to find what you are looking for and maybe even more.

business description

Our goal is to show users where and how they can watch the films they want to watch. We solve user problems such as: What to watch by showing users the trending content | Where to watch by showing users where their film is available | How to watch by show users how to purchase or rent the content they want to watch.

business portrait

We show you where you can legally watch movies that you love. You are kept up to date with what is new on their other streaming platforms. We curate content from streaming services which we then show to users.
